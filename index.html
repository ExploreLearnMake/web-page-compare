Compare web pages

Enter the URLs of two web pages that you wish to compare. After clicking on the Compare button, the differences will appear below. Items will be shown as in the examples below:

  • Items that were REMOVED from the oldest page will be shown in red with a strikethrough.
  • Items that were ADDED to the new page will be shown with a light green background and outline.
  • Items that were MODIFIED from the oldest to the newest page will be shown in yellow with a dashed underline.
